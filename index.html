Move On

Move On is a 2D Platformer made for the 2025 Winter GameJam held by the RWTH Aachen. The theme was "Nothing is permanent".


Your goal is to complete four different levels by collecting the flag at the end of each one. But dont wait for too long because only the fastest can make it onto the leaderboard!
