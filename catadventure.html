Cat Adventure

Cat Adventure is a Run and Gun Platformer Game where you play as a cat on a mission to catch the mouse at the end of each level. The game features over 30 levels across three different worlds, each with unique challenges and enemies. Test your skills in the final boss fight and prove you're the ultimate cat adventurer!
