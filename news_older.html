-You can now select between 5 different Cats to play with, which you can unlock during the game.

-Changed the size of some buttons to make it easier to click on them
-Changed some levels to make them more fun and easier to complete
-Changed the Select Level Menu to be better organized and to look better

S.A.C. release out now - 2020/07/30


S.A.C. Beta is released at the Google Play Store for Android and as a Zip file for Windows.

You are a Secret agent who works for the company S.A.C.! Your mission is to prevent a new company from building a deadly weapon. In order to do so you have to steal documents from their offices and take out some of their employees.
